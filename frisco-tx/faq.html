Costs & Pricing

Most Frisco homeowners pay between $750 and $1,800 for a complete Level 2 EV charger installation. This range covers the charger hardware, all wiring and conduit, panel circuit addition, mounting hardware, and labor. More complex jobs — such as running wire through a finished wall, installing in a detached garage, or upgrading a 100-amp panel — can cost more. We provide itemized, flat-rate quotes before any work begins.

Rebates & Incentives

Under the Inflation Reduction Act (IRA), homeowners can claim a 30% federal tax credit on the combined cost of EV charging equipment and installation labor, up to a maximum credit of $1,000 for residential installations. This credit directly reduces the federal income tax you owe — it is a true credit, not a deduction. Since Texas has no state income tax, this federal credit is the primary government incentive available to Frisco residents. We document your installation costs so your accountant has everything needed to claim the credit.

Rebate amounts and program availability can change on a quarterly basis. We track Oncor's current programs and apply for every rebate you qualify for as part of our standard service. Oncor rebates and the Federal 30% Tax Credit can be stacked, meaning you could potentially combine both incentives on the same installation.

The Federal 30% Tax Credit is available to all eligible Texas homeowners regardless of the lack of a state credit.

This can meaningfully reduce your monthly electricity bill, especially for EV owners who drive significant daily mileage.

Permits & Installation Process

Yes. The City of Frisco requires an electrical permit for any Level 2 (240V) EV charger installation. This is non-negotiable for both legal and insurance reasons — an unpermitted installation could void your homeowner's insurance or cause problems when selling your home. As licensed electricians, we handle 100% of the permitting process: application, fee payment, scheduling the City of Frisco inspection, and obtaining the final approval certificate. Permit fees are included in our quotes.
