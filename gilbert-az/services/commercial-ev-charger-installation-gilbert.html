For businesses, property owners, and fleet operators in Gilbert, AZ, commercial EV charging is no longer a perk. It is a competitive necessity.

Gilbert EV Charge specializes in commercial EVSE (Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment) design and installation. Whether you need 4 chargers for a small office parking lot or 40 chargers for a large retail center or apartment complex, we handle the complete project — from electrical load analysis and site design through permit management, installation, and system commissioning. We work with all major commercial EVSE network platforms, including ChargePoint, Blink, and EV Connect.

Types of Commercial EV Charger Installations We Handle

  • Office and Corporate Campus Parking — Employee-facing Level 2 chargers for office parks and corporate campuses. We design systems that can scale as your EV-driving workforce grows, using load management technology to avoid utility demand charges.
  • Retail and Restaurant Parking — Customer-facing charging stations that drive dwell time and attract EV-owning shoppers. ChargePoint and Blink networked units allow you to set pricing, monitor usage, and offer charging as a free or paid amenity.
  • Multifamily Residential (Apartments & Condos) — EV charging is one of the most-requested amenities for apartment and condo residents. We install both designated-space and shared-access charging systems, with smart billing solutions for individual tenant charging.
  • Hotels and Hospitality — Attract EV-driving guests and earn recognition from EV travel apps. We install lobby-adjacent and parking structure charging systems for hotels, resorts, and extended-stay properties.
  • Fleet Charging Depots — Overnight fleet charging for delivery vehicles, service fleets, and municipal EV fleets. We design high-capacity systems with load management to charge your entire fleet within your utility demand window.
  • Healthcare and Institutional Facilities — Accessible ADA-compliant charging stations for hospitals, medical offices, schools, and government facilities.

Our Commercial Installation Process

Commercial EV charger projects require more planning than residential installations. Here is how we approach every commercial project:

  1. Site Assessment & Load Analysis — We visit your property to assess available electrical capacity, transformer ratings, parking layout, and conduit routing options. We calculate your maximum load and determine whether a service upgrade, sub-panel, or load management system is required.
  2. System Design — We produce a complete design package including EVSE layout, electrical single-line diagrams, conduit routing plans, and equipment specifications. This package is used for permitting and contractor coordination.
  3. Permit Management — We handle all required permits through the Town of Gilbert (or the applicable jurisdiction), including electrical, civil, and any required ADA compliance documentation.
  4. Installation — Our licensed commercial electrical crews complete all wiring, conduit, panel work, mounting, and network commissioning. We coordinate with general contractors and property managers to minimize disruption to your operations.
  5. Network Configuration & Testing — We commission all networked EVSE units, configure pricing and access rules, set up your management portal, and test every charger before handoff.
  6. Incentive Filing — We identify and apply for all applicable incentives, including APS commercial EV charging programs and any applicable utility or state grants.
